Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Last Updated: 18 July 2025
At Kato Mine, accessible from https://KatoMine.com, we prioritize user privacy and data protection. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data in accordance with our secure registration and login system.

Information We Collect

We collect only the essential information needed to provide you with a secure and personalized experience on our platform:

Personal Information: Your name, email address, and securely encrypted password during registration
Account Recovery: Email address for password recovery and account verification through our 'Forgot Password' feature
User Identity: Unique username for platform identification and communication
Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve platform performance and user experience

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to enhance your experience and maintain platform security:

Verify and manage your account with advanced security protocols
Enable secure password reset through encrypted email verification
Personalize and optimize your mining experience on our platform
Provide customer support and technical assistance when needed
Our Commitment

We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data remains strictly confidential and is used only to provide you with the best possible service.

Cookies & Sessions

We use cookies and session management to enhance your experience:

Maintain secure login sessions for user convenience
Remember your preferences and settings
Analyze platform usage to improve performance

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit some platform functionalities.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Advanced encryption for all sensitive data transmission
Secure servers with regular security audits and updates
Multi-factor authentication options for enhanced account security
Important Security Note

While we implement robust security measures, no online system can guarantee 100% security. We encourage users to use strong, unique passwords and keep their login credentials confidential.

Third-Party Disclosure

We maintain strict privacy standards regarding data sharing:

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for marketing
Information may be disclosed only when required by law or legal process
Limited sharing with trusted service providers for platform maintenance only

Children's Privacy

Protecting children's privacy is a top priority for us:

We do not knowingly collect information from users under 18 years of age
Any accidentally collected minors' information is immediately deleted
Parents can contact us to review or remove their child's information

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements:

Users will be notified of significant policy changes via email
Updated policies become effective 30 days after notification
Continued platform use indicates acceptance of updated terms

Questions About Our Privacy Policy?

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please don't hesitate to contact us.
